Advanced tennis tactics most often overlooked

Entry level players

This is the case when the player can support modern game and he/she can return moderate and slow speed serves and attackes of the opponent. For this level of game the player needs knowledge of the basic forehand and backhand. The are various players who can do some fairy good game but still remains the base style players. When encounted with the advanced players they often loose game with no chance to win.

Advanced players

This level of game demonstrates the most players on advanced amateur circle.They can play a fairly good game and play very well as compaired to the ordinary people. To reach this kind of game you have to master most used tactics and technique but spent a lot of time practicing thier play.It is not easy to reach that level of game. What distinquishes these players are knowledge and practice that could only be learned at the tennis schools.

Professional play

This is close to professional tennis. The players in this category can do an excellent game and compete at the amateur tennis tournaments. The technique of these players are at the level that allow then to make shots from every spot on the court and deliver the ball to the targeted location. No doubt it take a lot of time and practice that should be regular.
